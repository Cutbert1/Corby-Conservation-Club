About Us

Our purpose is to bring nature back, to empower people to take meaningful action for nature, and to create an inclusive society where nature matters.


Our vision is of a thriving natural world, with our green initative, wildlife and natural habitats playing a valued role in addressing the climate and ecological emergencies, and everyone inspired to get involved in nature's recovery

Who we are

Corby Conservative Union are a grassroots movement of people from a wide range of backgrounds and all walks of life, who believe that we need nature and nature needs us. We have more than 150 members, over 1500 volunteers. We collaborate with charities, community interest groups, local councils each to form a groups of people getting together and working with others to make a positive difference to ecosystem and future generations, starting where we live and work.


What we do

For more than 4 years we have been campaigning for greener environment and saving wildlife, increasing people's awareness and understanding of the natural world, and deepening people's relationship with it.

We work closely with schools, colleges and universities, with farmers and landowners, fisher, divers and other environmental organisations in and around Northamptonshire.
